About

TheSizNexus is a private intelligence corporation built for a small, close-knit group of operatives.

What it is

TheSizNexus is a members-only platform where operatives run missions, climb clearance ranks, coordinate across squads, and earn Net — the corporation's internal currency. It's part social hub, part ops center, built from scratch with no off-the-shelf templates.

Who runs it

Founded and operated by $iz (ItzzzShxdow). The platform is actively maintained and developed. There is no corporate structure behind this — it's a one-person operation.

Tech stack

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks.
  • Auth & Database: Firebase Authentication + Firestore under project thesiznexus.
  • Hosting: Firebase Hosting at siznexus.org.

Joining

TheSizNexus is invite-only by culture, not by gate. You can create an account and start at Unaffiliated clearance. Rank is earned through activity, missions, and trust — not purchased or requested.
